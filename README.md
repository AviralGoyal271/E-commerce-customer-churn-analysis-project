# E-commerce Customer Churn Analysis

## Project Overview

Customer retention is a critical business objective, as acquiring new customers is significantly more expensive than retaining existing ones. This project analyzes customer behavior and churn patterns to identify key drivers influencing customer attrition and provides actionable business recommendations to improve retention and customer lifetime value.The analysis was performed using MySQL for data extraction and transformation, and Power BI for interactive dashboard development and business reporting.

---

## Business Problem

Organizations often experience customer churn without a clear understanding of the underlying causes. The absence of timely insights into customer behavior limits the effectiveness of retention strategies and impacts long-term revenue growth.This project addresses these challenges by analyzing customer demographics, engagement, purchasing behavior, and risk indicators to uncover meaningful business insights.

---

## Project Objectives

- Analyze customer demographics and behavioral patterns.
- Measure overall customer churn and retention performance.
- Identify high-risk customer segments.
- Understand factors contributing to customer churn.
- Develop an interactive dashboard for business stakeholders.
- Recommend data-driven strategies to improve customer retention.

## Key Business Insights

- High-risk customers exhibit lower engagement and higher inactivity.
- Customer spending and engagement strongly influence churn probability.
- Targeted retention strategies are required for different customer segments.
- Business KPIs reveal opportunities to improve customer loyalty and reduce revenue loss.

---

## Business Recommendations

- Launch proactive retention campaigns.
- Strengthen customer engagement initiatives.
- Introduce customer loyalty programs.
- Reduce inactivity through targeted promotions.
- Monitor churn KPIs regularly for continuous improvement.

---

## Expected Business Impact

- Improve customer retention
- Reduce churn rate
- Increase customer lifetime value (CLV)
- Enhance customer engagement
- Support data-driven business decisions
